QuickStore!

QuickStore Private Limited, launched in 2024, is an Indian e-commerce company, headquartered in Bangalore, and incorporated as a private limited company. Initially focused on online electronics sales, QuickStore expanded into diverse product categories including fashion, home essentials, groceries, lifestyle products, and more. The platform competes primarily with Amazon India, Flipkart, and domestic rival Paytm Mall. As of FY24, QuickStore holds a significant market share in the Indian e-commerce industry. With strategic acquisitions and partnerships, QuickStore maintains a strong presence in the fashion segment and consistently rivals top players in electronics and mobile sales.
